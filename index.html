Table of Cholera Attacks and Deaths by Date

One attack represents one individual contracting cholera on the specific date. One death represents one individual dying from cholera on the specific date.

Chart of Cholera Attacks and Deaths by Date

This line chart visualizes attacks and deaths over time, as well as cumulative attacks and cumulative deaths. One can identify the rapid spread and slow decline of the outbreak as well as the staggeringly high fatality rate.

Table of Age and Gender of Cholera Deaths per 10,000 People in 1800's Naples

This table shows data collected from an outbreak of cholera in Naples during years similar to the UK outbreak. Data is divided into age ranges and genders. Male and Female values represent deaths per 10,000 individuals.

Bar Chart of Age and Gender of Cholera Deaths per 10,000 People in 1800's Naples

This bar chart shows data collected from an outbreak of cholera in Naples during years similar to the UK outbreak. Data is divided into age ranges and genders. Male and Female values represent deaths per 10,000 individuals.

Pie of Gender Demographics for Cholera Deaths per 10,000 People in 1800's Naples

This pie chart shows the distribution of Male to Female deaths caused by cholera in Naples.

Map of Pumps and Death Data

This map shows the water pump locations. These purple points each represent a single pump. The dark blue circles represent deaths due to cholera. Larger blue circles represent more deaths, smaller circles represent less deaths.
